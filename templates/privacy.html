Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect basic information such as your name and email address. When you use Spendly, we also collect the expense records you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide. We do not collect payment card numbers or bank credentials.

We may also automatically collect certain technical data such as your browser type, IP address, and pages visited within the app to help us diagnose issues and improve the Service.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect to operate and improve Spendly. Specifically, we use it to authenticate your account, store and display your expense history, generate summaries and reports, and respond to support requests.

We do not use your financial data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond providing the Service to you.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us. We will process such requests within a reasonable timeframe.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may use third-party services for infrastructure (such as hosting providers) and analytics. These providers are contractually required to handle your data securely and only for the purposes we specify. We do not sell your data to any third party.

This policy does not cover third-party websites or services that may be linked from within Spendly. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

5. Cookies

Spendly uses session cookies strictly necessary to keep you signed in. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but doing so will prevent you from staying logged in to your account.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding your personal data or this Privacy Policy, please reach out through the Spendly support channel. We take privacy seriously and will respond promptly.
